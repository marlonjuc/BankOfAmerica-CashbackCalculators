*Earn Cash Rewards of 1% of new net retail purchase transactions (qualifying purchases less credits, returns, and adjustments) charged to the card each billing cycle. For each qualifying retail purchase, you will receive 2% cash back of the Net Purchase at participating Restaurants. The 2% cash back consists of your base rate of 1% of Net Purchases plus a bonus of 1%. For each qualifying retail purchase, you will receive 3% cash back of the Net Purchase at participating Gas Stations and Office Supply Stores. The 3% cash back consists of your base rate of 1% of Net Purchases plus a bonus of 2%. A purchase qualifies for the bonus if the merchant assigns it to the appropriate merchant category code. There is an annual cap of $250,000 on rewards earned at the rate of 3% at gas stations and office supply stores; any such purchases over $250,000 will earn the base rate of 1%. Earn unlimited Cash Rewards. There is no annual cap on rewards redemption.

**Loyalty Bonus. Earn an additional cash back bonus of 0.25% when your monthly net retail purchases equal or exceed $2,000. We calculate the monthly cash back payout on the last day of the calendar month (based on net retail purchases posted to the account during that month), and we award the cash back bonus on the first day of the following month. Please note that this monthly time frame will not coincide with your card Billing Cycle. We calculate total net purchases monthly from the first day of every month to the last day of every month. If your total net purchases for a given month equal or exceed $2,000, you earn the cash back bonus, calculated by multiplying your total net purchase amount by 0.0025. The total net purchase balance for the purpose of calculating the cash back bonus will revert to $0 on the first day of each month.
