*The Travel Rewards Visa Signature® Business credit card allows you to earn 1.5 Points per dollar of Net Purchases (as defined in the Program Rules). You can earn 3 Points (1.5 base points and 1.5 bonus points) per dollar on Purchases made through the Bank of America Travel Center, excluding insurance purchases which will earn 1.5 points.

**Loyalty Bonus. Earn 1.875 points when your monthly net retail purchases equal or exceed $2,000 (your 1.5 base points plus a 25% bonus of .375 points). We calculate the monthly point bonus on the last day of the calendar month (based on net retail purchases posted to the account during that month), and we award the point bonus on the first day of the following month. Please note that these monthly bonus calculations will not coincide with your card Billing Cycles. We calculate total net purchases monthly from the first day of every month to the last day of every month. If your total net purchases for a given month equal or exceed $2,000, you earn the point bonus, calculated by multiplying your total net purchase amount by 0.375. The total net purchase balance for the purpose of calculating the point bonus will revert to $0 on the first day of each month. Example: $2,000 in total monthly qualifying purchases = 750 bonus points ($2,000 x 0.375).

***5,000 First Use Bonus Points:First Use Bonus Points are awarded after the first purchase made within 90 days of account opening. Bonus Points are awarded to the Company Level account. Only one First Use Bonus Points will be awarded per Company.

****If you have an open Business Advantage or Business Fundamentals checking solution, you may receive the 10,000 points when you open a new Travel Rewards Visa Signature® Business credit card account, link your new card to the checking solution and make at least $500 in retail purchases with your card that post to your account within 60 days from account opening. Card bonus only given to first-time small business card customers who have an existing Business Fundamentals or Business Advantage checking solution. Having an existing, stand-alone Business Economy Checking or Business Advantage Checking account does not qualify for the bonus (except in AZ, GA and MA). Co-branded cards are not eligible. If eligible, only one bonus allowed per company and will be applied to the company account.
